Today News

Students, who have cleared the higher secondary special supplementary examination, can apply for admission to Diploma in Elementary Education.

The government has issued an order to conduct separate counselling for the students. Accordingly, the application forms will be issued at the District Institute of Education and Training (DIET), Ranipet, from July 25 to August 1 (except Sunday) from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m.

Students belonging to the general category can get the form on payment of cash of Rs. 500, while those belonging to SC/ST community should pay Rs. 250 in cash. The filled-in application forms should be submitted by 5 p.m. on September 1 at DIET, Ranipet.

The online counselling – single window system— for admission to first year would be held at DIET from 9 a.m. on August 8, according to a press release.

The filled-in applications should be submitted by 5 p.m. on September 1 at DIET, Ranipet



Union HRD minister Prakash Javadekar today welcomed the decision by the Supreme Court which cancelled the counselling conducted so far by private medical colleges in Madhya Pradesh for admissions in undergraduate medical course and ordered that a combined counselling be held afresh.


Welcoming the order, Javadekar said Supreme Court has today given a decision about Madhya Pradesh and tomorrow they are going to give a decision about Maharashtra and other states.
