About Us

Internet Banking is a full-service facility that allows you to do your banking with complete security from the comfort and safety of your home or office. Those features and benifits available on our system are as follows.


Features and Benifits

Prepaid: Prepaid top-ups available from your mobile device or computer anywhere or anytime. You never have to worry about running out of prepaid purchases(e.g. SMS, Data bundles of BIS) anymore - simply log onto your Internet or mobile banking profile and quickly top-up from your service provider anytime. What's more, you can set a prepaid purchase for a later date by using the Future-dated option, so it will be recharged on date set in the future. Alternatively, make sure you are constantly in touch by regulating regular top-ups with our Recurring top-up option.

Investments: Investment account holders with NetBank can freely access the information on NetBank without needing a linked transcational account. NetBank investment account holders now have the convinience to freely view their entire list of statements and accounts balance online, as well as benifit of being able to maintain that account online as well. They can do it anytime they please - 24/7 - without needing a linked transcational account, such as current and savings account.
